New Home Construction

New house plans are constantly being developed to suit modern wants and needs, so you'll find that many have the same in-demand features. Things like open layouts, spacious master suites, and large mudrooms are common these days, for example. But our new house plans are incredibly diverse and they don't necessarily check the same boxes. Home Additions

The modern living space décorated with artful rectangular curved panels adds comfort and elegance to the space. Indeed, the lines of the paneling are smooth and slim, which makes the atmosphere of the room warm and fluid. In addition to this classy setting, the room features a mustard-coloured sectional sofa to give the space a shot of colour

Bathroom Remodels

A contemporary bathroom is more lavish, minimalistic, and elegant, as demonstrated by the beige patterned tiles that bring the warm feeling. These tiles can effectively blend in with the surrounding environment, adapt to natural light, and create the illusion of a larger space. When installed together with the wall-mounted vanity, it contributes to using even more of floor space and eradicating cluttered feelings

Kitchen Remodels

A modern kitchen design incorporates vibrant colors and stone finish laminate shutters to create a dynamic and sophisticated atmosphere. The bright hues add a lively touch, bringing energy and warmth to the space, while the stone finish laminate shutters offer a sleek, contemporary look. This combination of bold colors and polished textures transforms it into an inviting area.

Windows & Doors

Every home, of course, has views of its surroundings, but not every home enjoys breathtaking landscapes or seascapes. Some do, however, and for those fortunate few, this collection of homes with Great Views will be appreciated. Each home’s smart design is coupled with an abundance of large windows or sprawling exterior decks to optimize the spectacular views, whether lakeside, mountainside, or on the beach.

Decks & Porches

A deck or porch construction plan should include detailed drawings of the structure's layout, framing, support beams, footings, ledger board attachment to the house, decking material, railings, stairs (if applicable), and specific dimensions, all while adhering to local building codes, considering factors like size, material selection, and desired design elements; it's crucial to accurately depict the location of posts, joists, beams, and how they connect to the house foundation, ensuring proper structural integrity and safety.
